# saas-churn-revenue-analysis
Analyzing SaaS churn drivers and revenue loss using Power BI
# 🔥 SaaS Revenue Collapse Analysis: Churn, Retention & $139K Revenue Risk

---

## 🚨 Problem Statement

A mid-market SaaS company is experiencing **stagnant revenue despite customer growth**.

This project investigates:
- Why ARR is not growing  
- What’s driving customer churn  
- Where revenue is leaking  
- How to recover lost revenue  

---

## 📊 Key Metrics Snapshot

| Metric | Value |
|------|------|
| Active Customers | 5.2K |
| Current ARR | $316.53K |
| Churn Rate | 26.6% |
| Revenue Lost | $139.1K |
| Revenue Retention | 69.5% |

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

- 🚨 Churn increased **10x (6% → 59%)**, signaling a severe retention breakdown  
- 📉 Revenue retention dropped from **92% → 32% (-60 percentage points)**  
- ⚠️ **59% of total revenue lies in high-risk segments** (high churn + high value)  
- ⏳ ~50% of churn occurs within the **first 6 months** → onboarding failure  
- 📅 Month-to-month contracts show **42.7% churn** vs **<12% for annual plans**  
- 🌐 Fiber customers churn **2x more than DSL users** → premium experience gap  
- 🛠️ Lack of tech support significantly increases churn  

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- ✅ Transition customers to **annual contracts** to stabilize revenue  
- ✅ Launch a **90-day onboarding success program**  
- ✅ Improve **Fiber product performance and pricing alignment**  
- ✅ Provide **priority support for Enterprise & Pro customers**  
- ✅ Shift focus from acquisition → **retention & expansion revenue**
